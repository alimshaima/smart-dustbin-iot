SMART DUSTBIN - COMPONENTS LIST

1. Arduino Uno R3 Compatible Board
   - Microcontroller: ATmega328
   - 14 Digital I/O Pins (6 PWM outputs)
   - 6 Analog Inputs
   - 16 MHz Ceramic Resonator

2. HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Distance Measuring Sensor
   - Operating Voltage: 5V DC
   - Sensing Range: 2cm to 400cm
   - Accuracy: 3mm

3. 9g Micro Servo Motor
   - Operating Voltage: 4.8V - 6V
   - Torque: 1.5kg/cm
   - Speed: 0.1 sec/60 degrees

4. Jumper Cables
   - Length: 20cm
   - Types: M-M, M-F, F-F
   - Quantity: 10 pieces each type

5. Power Supply
   - 9V Battery
   - Battery Snap Connector
   - DC Barrel Jack Adapter

6. Dustbin
   - Any size with hinged lid
